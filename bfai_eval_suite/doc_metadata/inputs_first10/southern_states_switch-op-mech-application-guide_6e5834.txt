Southern States Disconnect Switch Shipment Overview
> Disconnect Switches > GROUP OPERATED SWITCHES
A P P L I C A T I O N  G U I D E
All group operated disconnect switch shipments shall include some or all of the 
following items: Switch phase(s), operating mechanism (op-mech), component 
box, switch accessory box or crate, operating pipe, and large pieces of loose steel. 
The accessories box may contain: arcing horns, corona rings, corona balls, etc. 
Each crate, container, box, or loose item will be marked with the Southern States 
JO #, and JO item #. The operating pipe bundle will additionally be identified with 
the operating mechanism drawing #.
