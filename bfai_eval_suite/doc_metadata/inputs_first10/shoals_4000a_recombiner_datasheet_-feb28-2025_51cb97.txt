Key Notes:
Configurations with no Input-Side Disconnect or Input-Side Contactor are limited to 8 fuse pairs per cabinet. Configurations exceeding 8 fuse 
pairs will need additional cabinets.	
	
	
	
•	 1-8 fuse pairs = 2 cabinet
•	 9-16 fuse pairs = 3 cabinets
1) Input-Side Disconnect Option:
•	 Requires 3 cabinets 
•	 Input-Side Disconnects are in a separate cabinet from the fuse pairs
•	 Limited to a maximum of 8 fuse pairs 
•	 Available with 6 or 8 disconnects only
•	 Not available if Input-Side Contactor option is selected
2) Input-Side Contactor Option:
•	 Limited to 6 fuse pairs per cabinet. Configurations exceeding 6 fuse pairs will need additional cabinets
•	 1-6 fuse pairs = 2 cabinet
•	 7-12 fuse pairs = 3 cabinets	
•	 Not available if Input-Side Disconnect option is selected
